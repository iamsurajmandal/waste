Our Moto

Recycling Cops will be a leader in innovative solutions and strong advocacy for a sustainable waste recycling on individual, community or government level so that these resources could be available for our future generations also.

Why to chose Us ?
We work on the motto that the resources we are using today should be passed to our next generations in not only abundant quantity but in better quality than we have today also.
We use totally recycled plastic bags for pickup and no carbon emission vehicals for the pickup and transportation.
We also provide option of digital payment to our clients to reduce the use of paper currency.
Most of our employees are those who were rag pickers and beggars at a time so we provided them a mainstream job where they can earn a respectful livelihood.
